About me

I love writing code. Software engineering is never-ending puzzle that I am passionately engaged in solving. I love cooking.

Cooking can become a very personal way to happiness - a kind of meditation, fulfillment and happiness. Often the simple activities bring the greatest joy. Our life has become much faster, more stressful and hectic in recent years. That is why it is so important for me to come to rest, to relax and to refuel. I personally completely forget the time in the kitchen.
