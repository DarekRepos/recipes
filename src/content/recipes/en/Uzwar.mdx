## Uzvar from Plums and Berries (Dessert)

### Instructions:

1. Wash the plums, morello cherries, and raspberries thoroughly.
2. Place the washed fruit in a large pot and cover with water.
3. Add the sugar, honey, and raisins to the pot.
4. Simmer the ingredients slowly for about 1 hour.
5. Remove from heat and let the mixture sit for 4 hours to allow the fruit to swell and the flavors to meld together.
6. Serve chilled or at room temperature. The Uzvar may also be made with apples and cranberries for a different flavor.

## Ingredients

### Serves about 3:

- **100 dag** plums
- **100 dag** morello cherries
- **100 dag** raspberries
- **500 ml** water
- **186 g** sugar
- **Raisins** (quantity as desired)
- **Honey** (quantity as desired)
