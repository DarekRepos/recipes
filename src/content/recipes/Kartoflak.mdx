## Kartoflak

Grate the peeled potatoes and onion finely. Squeeze out excess fluid. Chop the bacon into small cubes and fry. Mix all the ingredients. Season to taste and place in a baking tin. Bake for approx. 1 hour.

## Ingredients

### Serves about 5

- **1 kg** potatoes
- **2** eggs
- **1** medium-sized onion
- **20 dag** smoked bacon
- **3 tbsp** wheat flour
- Salt, pepper to taste
